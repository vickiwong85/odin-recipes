Fruit Sando

Description

Fruit Sandos are Japanese fruit sandwiches with whipped cream. They are lovely and light treats that have a beautiful cross-section with a variety of fruits.

This recipe makes 4 servings in 10 min.

Ingredients

  • 4 Slices of white bread
  • Whipping/fresh cream
  • Fruit of your liking

Steps

  1. Whisk whipping/fresh cream with sugar (to form whipped cream)
  2. Spread cream on one side of a slice of bread
  3. Arrange fruits on top of bread
  4. Fill in any gaps between the fruits with the whipped cream. Then, place the other slice of bread on top of it
  5. Wrap the sandwich tightly with plastic wrap. Mark the cutting direction
  6. Put it in the refrigerator to allow it to set its shape
  7. With a knife, cut it diagonally *DO NOT take off the plastic wrap when cutting
